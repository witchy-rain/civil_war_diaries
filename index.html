A Collection and Analysis of Civil War Letters

Project Statement

 The project's objective is to collect and analyze the letters of both Confederate and Union soldiers. We scrutinized the content to explore the emotions of the soldiers expressed in those letters within that historical timeframe. These sentiment attributes include:

  • bittersweet
  • happy
  • sad
  • angry
  • afraid
  • disgusted
  • hopeful
  • hopeless
  • triumphant
  • defiant
  • defeated
