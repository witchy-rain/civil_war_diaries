Project Mission

This project's goal is to gather both confederate and union soldier letters, and analyze their letters for the according emotions:

  • bittersweet
  • happy
  • sad
  • angry
  • afraid
  • disgusted
  • hopeful
  • hopeless
  • triumphant
  • defiant
  • defeated
